Digital Printing

  • Paper Stock
    • Paper stock is one of the most important aspects in printing. You will need
    the correct paper stock for your project. And since each project is different,
    we can provide you with all the different paper stock you need.

    • Glossy
      • 70 GSM to 400 GSM
      • Single Sided or Double Sided
      • 12" x 18", A1, A2, A3, A4
    • Matte
      • 50 GSM to 500 GSM
      • Single Sided or Double Sided
      • 12" x 18", A1, A2, A3, A4
    • Textured

  • Ink
    • Another important element of printing is ink. At Press Café we use only
    quality ink purchased from the machine brands we utilize. These are HP,
    Epson, Canon, and Kodak – all of which are highly reputable suppliers of
    colored and black & white ink all over the world.

    We will guarantee that our ink does not run, smear, blotch. It also does
    not stick to other printed pages unlike the result in other digital
    printing presses.

      • Photo
      • Vivera
    • Epson
      • Emulsified
      • Embossed
      • Flat
    • Canon & Kodak
      • Photo

  • Borderless
    • Our latest printers have now the capability to print borderless. That means
    more space for your designs and documents. You may print photos, or documents
    borderless without any worry. The strip-side technology we installed in all
    our printers now ensures crisp edges, firm, and not-torn printed edges.
